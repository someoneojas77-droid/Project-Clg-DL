# Stay Safe Online — Prevention Checklist for College Students

1. Never enter your UPI PIN to RECEIVE money — PIN is only needed 
   to SEND money.
2. Always verify the identity of the caller before acting on any 
   payment request.
3. Never share your OTP, UPI PIN, or CVV with anyone — not even 
   bank representatives.
4. Enable transaction limits on your UPI app to minimise potential loss.
5. Check the UPI ID carefully before approving any collect request — 
   fraudsters use IDs that look similar to real ones.
6. Use only official apps (PhonePe, GPay, Paytm) downloaded from 
   the Play Store or App Store — never third-party links.
7. Enable two-factor authentication on all your accounts and apps.
8. Never click on links received via SMS or WhatsApp claiming to be 
   from your bank or UPI provider.
9. Regularly check your bank statements for any unauthorised 
   transactions.
10. If you are selling something online, never trust a buyer who 
    insists on paying only via UPI collect request.

## If You Are a Victim — Report Immediately:
- National Cyber Crime Portal: cybercrime.gov.in
- Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 (24x7, toll-free)
- Your Bank: Call immediately to freeze the transaction
