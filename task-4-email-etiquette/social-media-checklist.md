# Social Media Do's and Don'ts for College Students

## Do's
1. Use a professional username across all platforms.
2. Think before you post — ask yourself if you'd be comfortable 
   with your professor or employer seeing it.
3. Keep your LinkedIn and GitHub profiles updated and professional.
4. Use privacy settings to control who sees your personal posts.
5. Give credit when sharing someone else's work or ideas online.

## Don'ts
1. Never share your passwords or OTPs with anyone, including friends.
2. Don't post offensive, discriminatory, or inappropriate content.
3. Avoid sharing your location, address, or daily routine publicly.
4. Don't engage in or encourage cyberbullying of any kind.
5. Never spread unverified news or misinformation online.
